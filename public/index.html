Features

A portfolio optimizer built for insurance risk

Our goal is to bring the best of breed analytics to insurance professional’s fingertips to drive actionable insight quickly and easily. This is not an app built to handle all types of financial risk with insurance portfolios as an after thought.

Manage the tail

We have built an app from the ground up specifically for the insurance industry. We started with how the app measures risk. We have stepped away from standard deviations used in other financial analysis tools and focused on analytics using risk measures developed by the insurance industry. Specifically, risk measures described by actuaries as Coherent Distortion Risk Measures (CDRM) which includes Value-at-Risk, Tail Value-at-Risk, and Wang Transforms among others.

Manage Exposure Concentrations

Spatial concentration is a much bigger focus in the insurance industry, and it is a big focus of ours as well. Insurance providers have a limited ability to segment risks by geography because a policy offering coverage for only certain geographies to large complex risks through either primary insurance, reinsurance, or catastrophe bonds would not be competitive with a complete offering. Our app can determine what size positions to take while respecting your specified spatial aggregation rules. You’ll save expenses by meeting your objectives without having to transfer away excess risk on the back end.

Complements Capital Allocation Based Analysis

Capital allocations can enable a host of rate of return type metrics useful for management and our app can complement that by helping to determine directions the business to take to maximize overall return while minimizing the combined tail risk of the overall company.

Platform Agnostic

Whether you are using AIR or RMS modeling results, the app will be able to analyze results from either of these major models. We are also working on being able to analyze portfolios with mixed modeling results where risks in the portfolio are not consistently modeled with one catastrophe model.

Fast Results

Speed is an important aspect of any analysis. Our app incorporates considerable research from both a mathematics perspective and an infrastructure perspective to be able to deliver the analytics you need fast.

  • The mathematical models used to generate results has been refined to do so in the quickest possible manner. This makes finding the optimal solution among tens of thousands of simulations across all of the risks in your portfolio while respecting your custom geographical definitions within minutes rather than days.
  • The app is powered by cloud based computing that can scale appropriately for the size of your portfolio so that you can continue to get fast results as the size and complexity of your portfolio increases.
  • The features of the analytical engine are also available by API so that the app can provide its results to your existing reporting and analytical infrastructure.

How It Works

  1. Upload simulation results
  2. Specify your choice of risk measure and target rate of return
  3. Specify contract rules - the maximum, minimum, or fixed amounts by contract
  4. Specify group rules (geography, underwriting groups, lines of business)- the maximum and/or minimum concentration allowed
  5. The app returns the optimal portfolio that minimizes your tail risk for the given targeted return as well as other relevant analytics within minutes
